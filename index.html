About KC Soaps

Founded in 2025 by Karri Cummings, KC Soaps is dedicated to crafting high-quality, handmade soaps and lotions. Each item is made with care, using natural ingredients and thoughtful combinations of essential oils to support healthy skin and a soothing self-care routine.

Karri started KC Soaps out of her passion for skincare and creativity. What began as a small project in her home has grown into a brand focused on quality, sustainability, and handmade charm.
